# Judging Criteria

## The Task: The meeting that shouldn't exist

Tackle meeting overload. Help teams decide whether a meeting is actually needed, who should be there, and what could have been handled asynchronously.

## Judging Criteria

### 1. Problem Fit & Insight

Does the team understand _why_ bad meetings happen, and solve a specific failure mode, not "meetings are bad"?

### 2. Decision Quality & Workflow Integration

Does it change behavior at the moment of decision? Does it show up where work happens (calendar, Slack, the invite flow) and offer a real async alternative?

### 3. Measurable Impact

Can the team show the win? Hours saved, meetings prevented, attendees reduced, decisions made faster.

_Bonus for honest counterfactuals._
